Quick Feet (ability)

Effect

Quick Feet raises the ability-bearer's Speed by 50% when inflicted by a major status ailment (poison, burn, paralysis, freeze or sleep). If paralyzed, the PokÃ©mon does not suffer the usual drop in Speed.
