Effects

Feint Attack deals damage and ignores changes to the Accuracy and Evasion stats. However, it will not hit PokÃ©mon during the invulnerable stage of Bounce, Dig, Dive, Fly, Shadow Force or Sky Drop.

Changes

In Generations 2-5, this move's name was spelled Faint Attack.
