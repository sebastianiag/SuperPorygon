Effects

Acrobatics deals damage, however, if the user is not holding an item, its power doubles to 110.

If the user is holding a Flying Gem, it is consumed before the Pokemon attacks, so it receives both boosts and increases Acrobatics' power to 165. Once the Gem is consumed Acrobatics' power for the rest of the battle will be 110.
