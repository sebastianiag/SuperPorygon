Introduced Generation 3

Effects

Spit Up deals varying damage depending on how many times the user used Stockpile. The base power is 100, 200 or 300 if the user stockpiled one, two or three times respectively.

Spit Up also removes the Defense and Special Defense boosts given through Stockpile.
