Effects

Captivate lowers the Special Attack of the target by two stages, if they are of the opposite gender to the user. Stats can be lowered to a minimum of -6 stages each.

Captivate will fail if both Pokemon are the same gender, either Pokemon is genderless, or the target has the ability Oblivious.
