Effects

Wide Guard protects the user's team from all multi-target moves for that turn, such as Surf or Earthquake. Since its priority is +3 it will be used before all damage-dealing moves.

Its accuracy drops to 50% if used consecutively after itself, Detect, Protect, Endure or Quick Guard.
