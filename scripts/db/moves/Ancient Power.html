Move data

Type Rock
Category Special Special
Power 60
Accuracy 100
PP 5  (max. 8)
Introduced Generation 2

Effects

Ancient Power deals damage and has a 10% chance of raising all the user's stats at once - Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense and Speed - by one stage each.

Stats can be raised to a maximum of +6 stages each.

Evolution

The following Pokemon evolve after learning Ancient Power:

  • Tangela (evolves to Tangrowth) - learnt at level 33, 36 or 40 in different games.
  • Yanma (evolves to Yanmega) - learnt at level 33.
  • Piloswine (evolves to Mamoswine) - learnt via Move Relearner

Changes

In Generations 2-5, this move's name was formatted as AncientPower.
