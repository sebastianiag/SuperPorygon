Effects

If it hits, Horn Drill is guaranteed to make the opponent faint. It is more likely to hit PokÃ©mon that are of a lower level than the user.

Its accuracy (as a percentage) is calculated as below, ignoring all other accuracy and evasion modifiers:

UserLevel - TargetLevel + 30

This means that Horn Drill will fail against a target PokÃ©mon at least 30 levels above the user.

Changes

In Generation 1, Horn Drill has 30% accuracy, but does not affect opponents faster than the user.
