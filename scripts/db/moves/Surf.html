Effects

Surf deals damage. In double battles it will hit all PokÃ©mon on the field; in triple battles it will hit all adjacent PokÃ©mon.

If the opponent is hidden underwater from using Dive, Surf will hit with double power.

Changes

In Generations 1-5, Surf has base power 95.

Outside of battle

Surf is used to cross water. While surfing, wild PokÃ©mon may appear. It can only be used with the appropriate Gym Badge.

From Generation 3 onward it's possible to fish while surfing; previously one could only fish at the water's edge.
