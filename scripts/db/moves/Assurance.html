Move data

Type Dark
Category Physical Physical
Power 60
Accuracy 100
PP 10  (max. 16)
Introduced Generation 4

Effects

Assurance deals damage, and if the target has already taken damage in this turn its power doubles to 120.

Changes

In Generations 4-5, Assurance has base power 50.
