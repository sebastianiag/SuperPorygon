Effects

Uproar inflicts damage for 3 turns. During this time, no PokÃ©mon on the field will be able to sleep, and any sleeping PokÃ©mon will be woken up.

Changes

In Generations 1-4, Uproar has a base power of 50 and lasts 2-5 turns.
