Move data

Type Normal
Category Special Special
Power 90
Accuracy 100
PP 10  (max. 16)
Introduced Generation 3

Effects

Hyper Voice deals damage with no additional effect, besides targeting multiple foes. Hyper Voice has no effect on Pokemon with the ability Soundproof.
