Effects

Detect prevents any attacks targeted at the user from striking on that turn. It is identical to Protect.

Its accuracy drops to 50% if used consecutively after itself, Protect, Endure, Quick Guard or Wide Guard.

Changes

In Generations 1-4, the accuracy of Detect drops by 50% each time when used consecutively after itself, Protect or Endure.
