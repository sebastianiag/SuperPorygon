Move data

Type Fighting
Category Physical Physical
Power 70
Accuracy 100
PP 10  (max. 16)
Introduced Generation 4

Effects

Wake-Up Slap deals damage, and hits with double power (140) if the target is asleep. However, it also wakes up the target.

Changes

In Generations 4-5, Wake-Up Slap has base power 60.
