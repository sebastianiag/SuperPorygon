Effects

Thunderbolt deals damage and has a 10% chance of paralyzing the target.

Electric type PokÃ©mon, those with the ability Limber or those behind a Substitute cannot be paralyzed.

Changes

In Generations 1-5, Thunderbolt has base power 95.
