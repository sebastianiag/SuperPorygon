Type Water
Category Status Status
Power -
Accuracy -
PP 20  (max. 32)
Introduced Generation 4

Effects

The user of Aqua Ring surrounds itself with a veil of water, which restores 1⁄16 of its maximum HP at the end of each turn.

If the user is holding a Big Root, Aqua Ring restores 30% more HP each turn - approximately 1⁄12 of its maximum.
