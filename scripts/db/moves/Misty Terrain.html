Effects

Misty Terrain creates a low-lying mist for 5 turns, that protects all grounded PokÃ©mon from status conditions (including confusion). It also halves the power of Dragon type attacks while in effect.

Grounded PokÃ©mon are simply those that are not raised. Raised PokÃ©mon are any Flying types or those under the effects of Levitate, Magnet Rise or Telekinesis. Full details in glossary.

Misty Terrain is a new terrain condition and can coexist at the same time as weather conditions (but not other terrain conditions).

The move Nature Power turns into Moonblast under Misty Terrain.
