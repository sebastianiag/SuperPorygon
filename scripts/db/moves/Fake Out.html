Move data

Type Normal
Category Physical Physical
Power 40
Accuracy 100
PP 10  (max. 16)
Priority +3
Introduced Generation 3

Effects

Fake Out attacks with a priority of +3 and causes the target to flinch if it hits. It is only successful on the first turn the Pokemon is in battle, but reset each time the Pokemon switches out and back in.

PokÃ©mon with the ability Inner Focus or those behind a Substitute cannot be made to flinch.

Changes

In Generations 1-4, Fake Out has priority +1.
