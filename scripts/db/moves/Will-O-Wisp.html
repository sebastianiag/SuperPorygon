Effects

Will-O-Wisp causes the target to become burned, if it hits. Burned PokÃ©mon lose 1⁄8 of their maximum HP each turn and their Attack is decreased by 50%.

Fire type PokÃ©mon, those with the ability Water Veil or those behind a Substitute cannot be burned.

In Generations 3-5, Will-O-Wisp has 75% accuracy.
