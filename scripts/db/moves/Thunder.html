Effects

Thunder deals damage and has a 30% chance of paralyzing the target.

Electric type PokÃ©mon, those with the ability Limber or those behind a Substitute cannot be paralyzed.

If used during rain, its accuracy rises to 100%. Conversely, if used during intense sunlight, its accuracy drops to 50%.

Thunder can hit Pokemon using Fly and Bounce, the latter dealing double damage.

Changes

In Generations 1-5, Thunder has base power 120.

In Diamond/Pearl only, Thunder has a 30% chance of breaking through Protect or Detect during rain.

In Generation 1, the chance of paralysis is 10%.
