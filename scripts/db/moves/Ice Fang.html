Effects

Ice Fang deals damage, has a 10% chance of freezing the target and has a 10% chance of causing the target to flinch (if the target has not yet moved).

Ice type PokÃ©mon, those with the ability Magma Armor or those behind a Substitute cannot be frozen. PokÃ©mon with the ability Inner Focus or those behind a Substitute cannot be made to flinch.
