Move data

Type Fighting
Category Physical Physical
Power 130
Accuracy 90
PP 10  (max. 16)
Introduced Generation 1

Effects

High Jump Kick deals damage, however, if it misses the user keeps going and crashes, losing 1⁄2 of its maximum HP. High Jump Kick cannot be used if Gravity is in effect.

Changes

In Generations 1-3 High Jump Kick has a base power of 85. In Generation 4 its base power is 100.

In Generation 1, the crash damage is always 1 HP. From Generation 2 to Generation 4, the crash damage is 1⁄2 of the damage the attack would have inflicted (which could be all of the user's own HP).

In Generations 1-4, High Jump Kick has 20 PP.

In Generations 1-5, this move's name was formatted as Hi Jump Kick.
