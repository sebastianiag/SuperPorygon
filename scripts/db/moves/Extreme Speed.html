Move data

Type Normal
Category Physical Physical
Power 80
Accuracy 100
PP 5  (max. 8)
Priority +2
Introduced Generation 2

Effects

Extreme Speed deals damage and attacks before the majority of other moves (priority of +2).

Changes

In Generations 1-4, its priority is +1, which means that an opponent using other priority moves such as Quick Attack or Aqua Jet can attack before Extreme Speed if the opponent is faster.

In Generations 2-5, this move's name was formatted as ExtremeSpeed.
