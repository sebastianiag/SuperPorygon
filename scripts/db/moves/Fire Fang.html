Effects

Fire Fang deals damage, has a 10% chance of burning the target and has a 10% chance of causing the target to flinch (if the target has not yet moved).

Fire type PokÃ©mon, those with the ability Water Veil or those behind a Substitute cannot be burned. PokÃ©mon with the ability Inner Focus or those behind a Substitute cannot be made to flinch.
